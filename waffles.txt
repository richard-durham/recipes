Ingredients:
- 3 cups of whole wheat, self raising, flour
- 2 medium bananas
- 1 cup frozen blueberries
- 2 medium eggs
- 2 cups milk
- 

Directions:
1. Add the frozen blueberries, milk, eggs, and bananas, to a blender.  Blend until nearly smooth.

2. Pour mixture from blender into a large bowl.

3. Add flour and mix.

4. Heat up waffle iron.  Once heated make sure to spead cooking oil onto the plate so that the waffles don't stick

5. Pour suitable amount of mixture onto the waffle plate and let cook, usually only about a minute - although the time can vary so be sure to check.

6. Serve while hot with maple syrup or Apple sauce.
